 How to Speed Up Traffic (Immediate Actions)
To get traffic within days instead of waiting months for Google SEO, you should immediately promote your tools where people are actively looking for them:

A. Submit to Alternative & Tool Directories (First 2 weeks)
These sites rank well and will send high-intent visitors immediately:

AlternativeTo.net: Suggest "HelloTools" as a free, privacy-friendly alternative to ads-heavy calculator sites.
Product Hunt: Launch your suite of 50+ privacy-first utility tools.
BetaList / StartupBase: Great for initial discovery traffic.
B. Answer High-Traffic Questions (Quora & Reddit)
People constantly ask questions that require your calculators (e.g., "How do I calculate my loan EMI?", "How to calculate chronological age?", "How to convert hex to binary?").

Search Reddit and Quora for these questions.
Provide a helpful, detailed answer, and add a link: "You can calculate this instantly and privately here: hellotools.net/tools/emi-calculator".
C. Build Backlinks (Crucial for Domain Authority)
Google ranks tools based on how many other sites link to them.

Share your codebase or tool suite on GitHub (with a link back to your live site).
Write short, informative articles on platforms like Medium, Dev.to, or Hashnode about math/finance calculators, linking to your tools.
Your site is technically perfect. Now, the main engine of growth is letting the internet know it exists!
